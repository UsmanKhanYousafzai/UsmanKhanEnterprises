Usman Khan Yousafzai is the Founder, CEO and Director of Usman Khan Enterprises (Pvt.) Ltd., a privately registered multi-sector enterprise operating across Pakistan and Spain.

Founded in 2023, the company has grown to encompass eight active ventures spanning commerce, real estate, technology, consulting, international trade, investments, hospitality and logistics. Operating with a dual presence between South Asia and Europe, Usman has built a company defined by founder-led decision-making, cross-border thinking, and a long-term approach to building institutions rather than chasing short-term gains.
